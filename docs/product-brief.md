# Document Aim
 The aim of this document is to outline quite a high level specification of what we are trying to achieve with this project. 

 This doc is not meant to include:
    - Complex Technical Architecture
    - Specific implementation details
    - Step by step guides of how we will improve or iterate the product.

# What are we trying to build?
At a high level we are trying to build a platform to enable people to track what culture and media they consume, suggest specific media for them and to build a better understanding of their likes and dislikes. 

This is to fill a hole in my current "cultural sphere" where I struggle to have a place to track what I consume but also find what I might enjoy next. 

This is an ambitious spec but ultimately this the aim for the end goal - though it will take many iterations to get there.

# Who is this for?
People who have an interest in understanding what culture (films, TV, games and books) they enjoy and don't enjoy - as well giving people a space to see what they consume and how often they consume things.
